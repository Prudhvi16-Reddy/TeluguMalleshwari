For the girl who lights up my universe

You are my everything, far beyond anything this world could offer,
A star that shines the brightest in the night sky,
In a universe full of wonders, my heart beats for you above all,
Each pulse echoing the beauty of your soul, a celestial lullaby.

You are the most precious treasure in my life, cherished beyond measure,
Like a rare jewel hidden in the depths of the cosmos,
Of all the gifts this world has to give, your love is the one I hold closest,
A gentle breeze in spring, soothing and warm as it flows.

You are the most beautiful blessing in my life, eclipsing all else,
A radiant sun illuminating my darkest days,
In this vast expanse of existence, my heart has chosen you as its one true home,
As the moon dances with the tides, so do I in your loving gaze.

In the tapestry of my life, your love is the brightest thread woven throughout,
An intricate pattern of laughter, joy, and sweet embrace,
You hold a place in my heart that no one else can ever occupy,
Like the changing seasons, forever etched in time and space.

A song just for you
